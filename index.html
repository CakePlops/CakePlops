What Are Cake Pops?


It's pretty simple, and pretty delicious.

Who Are You?

My name's Felicia, and I have been baking cake pops since 2012. I've made pops for birthday parties, bridal showers, and community service events as a friend and family member, and now I'm bringing my foods to the public.
